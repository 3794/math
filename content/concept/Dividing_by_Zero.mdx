# Dividing by Zero

## What is Dividing by Zero?

Dividing by Zero is a mathematical operation that is not defined.

## Why is Dividing by Zero not defined?

$$
\begin{align}
\frac{1}{0.1} &= 10 \\
\text{} \\
\frac{1}{0.01} &= 100 \\
\text{} \\
\frac{1}{0.00000001} &= 100000000 \\
\text{} \\
\frac{1}{0} &= +\infty \\
\text{} \\
\frac{1}{-0.1} &= -10 \\
\text{} \\
\frac{1}{-0.01} &= -100 \\
\text{} \\
\frac{1}{-0.00000001} &= -100000000 \\
\text{} \\
\frac{1}{0} &= -\infty \\
\text{} \\
\frac{1}{0} &= \text{undefined}
\end{align}
$$
